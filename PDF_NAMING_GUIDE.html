Naming Convention Pattern

Main Publications:

FirstAuthorLastNameYYYY_ShortDescriptiveTitle.pdf

Examples: - Naimi2024_PRNG.pdf (short acronym) - Naimi2018_SuperLearning.pdf (descriptive title) - Rudolph2023_AspirinPregnancy.pdf (topic-based)

AJE Classroom Articles: Same pattern, but stored in classroom/ subdirectory

Tips: - Use camelCase for multi-word titles (e.g., SuperLearning, MediationDisparities) - Keep titles concise but descriptive - Avoid special characters (except underscore _) - Always include year
